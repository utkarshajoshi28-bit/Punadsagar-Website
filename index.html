About Company

Punadsagar Farmers Producer Company Limited is an active non-government private limited company based in Nashik, Maharashtra.

The company was incorporated on 28 September 2021 to engage in agriculture, horticulture and market gardening activities.

It supports local farmers through collective aggregation, seed production, quality farming practices and direct market linkages.
